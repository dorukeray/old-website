Story

My Story - begins in 2004

Just a kid? Wait!

I was so curious that my mom had to teach me how to read and write when I was 3. This was the start in my life. I loved tinkering with things, to understand how they work and how to build them. I've always been dreaming to be an inventor since then.

When I started elementary school, I spent my whole time with reading about history and drawing the moments, battle scenes, maps which I just read. This was exciting until I realized that how tables can turn so quickly. Today I may be victorious, glorious; but tomorrow maybe defeated, perished.

New beginnings...

I started programming when I was 10, and this was a milestone in my journey. You write the code, and let everyone use it. What you can do is only limited by your skills and your imagination. My secondary school years were spent on personal projects.

After a boring exam year, I entered Vefa Lisesi, a historical high school in Istanbul. Then I discovered the Web, and started writing for it. Incredible. There also I met Berk, who is a fellow of Dorkodu.

Dorkodu: Revolutions

Dorkodu was born when I first branded all my work under the name of "Dorkodu", I was in secondary school then. The name comes from "Doruk" + "code".

When I saw the truth behind the Web, I asked this question myself : Do we really get the pure and true information from Web? No. All we get is some pages that others created. There is no chance to get what/how exactly you want.

What if we had a universal knowledge resource that we can find, discover and exchange only the true, meaningful and useful information? The search results would be pure information pieces connected to each other, gathered from the whole accumilation of open human knowledge.

This is what I dedicated the first part of my career to.
